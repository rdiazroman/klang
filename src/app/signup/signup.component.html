We aim to share our philosophie of life with you and take you with us on a musical trip where memories are kept in our hearts because they are shared.

Team

Dani Posada

As one of the founders of Klang Heimlich, Dani Posada is taking care of the bookings, logistics, decoration and artwork. Moreover, he is one of the three residents at Klang Heimlich and co-founder of other labels in Switzerland. Despite his young DJ career, Dani has already achieved to play in different cities around the world and is a well-known DJ to the Zurich nightlife scene. His sets as well as current productions are defined by groovy sounds that can go from downtempo over deep- and tech-house to techno, telling a story that is slowly building up and gaining in intensity until it reaches the climax.

Marc Bach

a.k.a. Papa Bach began his DJ career back in 1994. Since then he became an institution in the Swiss nightlife, not only as a DJ and promoter of different labels, but also as producer and owner of two different clubs in Zurich, Pfingstweide and Cafe Gold. His background as well as his close friendship to the three founders of Klang Heimlich led him to join the Family in 2017. Since then, Marc is in charge of finance, staff management and logistics. He is also one of the Klang Heimlich residents, playing together with his partner Patrick Juen, under the name Juen & Bach. This duo is well known within and beyond the Swiss borders for their great music.

Phil Lutz

He is the only Family member that is not behind the dj-booth during the events but his passion for music has no boundaries and is always reflected in the Klang Heimlich lineup. Phil has been in the Swiss nightlife scene for more than 15 years with very successful events in different cities. His experience is a very valuable asset for each single event that we create and has enabled us to create new worlds from scratch. Phil has THE eye for unique locations and takes the lead to transform them in successful events.

Tom Hängs

One of the three founding fathers of the Klang Heimlich crew. He enriches the Family with skills in graphics, logistics and technics. He enjoys redefining the musical boundaries of Klang Heimlich events with “out of the box” bookings. His wide range of musical taste is reflected in his sets, the longer the better: from melodic electronica, Tom glides effortlessly into the deeper shades of house and organic layers of techno all the while adding a twist of dub but never losing grip of a solid groove. Always good for a surprise, his playful persona endears the crowds minds, bodies and souls
