Mindset

The passion for the music, the sense of community and unconditional love for our neighbours, losing track of time while dancing and the creation of new worlds from scratch in unique locations represent the pillars of Klang ♫ Heimlich. We aim to share our philosophie of life with you and take you with us on a musical trip where memories are kept in our hearts because they are shared.

Artists

Dani Posada

As one of the founders of Klang Heimlich, Dani Posada is taking care of the national bookings, decoration and artwork. Moreover, he is one of the three residents at Klang Heimlich. His sets are defined by groovy sounds that can go from downtempo over deep- and techhouse to techno, telling a story that is slowly building up and gaining in intensity until it reaches the climax.
